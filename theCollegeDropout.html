The College Dropout

Artist: Kanye West

Released: February 10, 2004

Genre: Hip-Hop

Label: Roc-A-Fella, Def Jam

Description: The College Dropout is Kanye West's debut studio album. It features a blend of soul samples and innovative production, addressing themes of education, self-consciousness, and perseverance. The album includes hit singles like "Through the Wire," "Jesus Walks," and "All Falls Down."
